ABOUT..

QA Portal :

QA portal is an effort to help its members in getting quick answers to the questions they have, regarding various topics from the Computer Science Environment.

It is built with a motive of identifying the key area's of interest, and providing a media to build up on their interests ..

Several features supported by the system are:

  • To enable its members to ask questions on various topics listed.

  • To enable members to spread their knowledge in the field by providing answers to the other members post's.

  • To enable its members to Judge the appropriateness of the answer and vote in favour or against the comments.

  • It provides a mailing service to its members, if he/she wants to mail the question to his id, he is allowed to do so. Moreover, periodic mails will be sent to the members on the topics of interest, if they want to avail the facility.

  • Members with maximum votes every month will find their photo on the main page of the site, this is to encourage members to help others.

  • Anyone can view the current members kist and get their details.

  • Teachers who are member's of this Site can send accross important information to the students, which can be recieved by the members through mail or through the site.

  • The most importanat feature introduced here is that if any member wants to know the interview process of a company, he/she can get it by just tagging the companies name, the system will inturn send a mail to the seniours who are placed in that company, reply can be seen through mail service or through the website, this can be availed only by the 6th, 7th and 8th semester students.
